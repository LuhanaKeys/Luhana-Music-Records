About me

Music Producer

I am Enoch Luhana, Stage name: Luhana Keys, Creator of Luhana Music Records. I am a music producer who began my journey while I was in 7th Grade. I take inspiration from artists like Metro Boomin, Central Cee and producer like Jay Cactus. From this, I took inspiration and began to make my own beats. I firstly started using LMMS as my DAW but eventually graduated to FL Studio. I have explored many other DAWs but according to my research, FL Studio is best at doing what it does because of its quick tools that make the music production process an interesting one. I began making Drill beats, due to heavy popularity of Drill during the COVID-19 period which was around the time I began making beats. I eventually started making trap beats around 9th Grade and RnB in 10th.
